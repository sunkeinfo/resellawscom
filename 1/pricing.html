Frequently Asked Questions

What is AWS Resell?

AWS Resell allows you to purchase AWS services through ResellAWS.com at discounted rates while receiving expert support and guidance.

Is joining ResellAWS.com free?

Yes, joining our AWS reseller program is completely free with no setup costs or hidden fees.

What does "switching billing" mean?

You'll receive AWS invoices through ResellAWS.com instead of directly from AWS, enabling discounts and additional benefits.

Will ResellAWS.com access my AWS account?

No, we only handle billing. You maintain full control and access to your AWS resources and services.
